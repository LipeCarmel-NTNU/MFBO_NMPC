\section*{Definition of the tuning vector $\theta$}

The NMPC configuration is parametrized by the column vector
\[
\theta^\top =
\big[
\mathrm{Iter}_{\max},\;
\theta_p,\;
\theta_m,\;
q,\;
r^{(u)},\;
r^{(\Delta u)}
\big].
\]

The components satisfy:
\begin{align*}
\mathrm{Iter}_{\max} &\in \{1,2,\ldots,300\}, \\
\theta_m < 30 &\in \mathbb{Z}_{\ge 0}, \\
\theta_p < 60 &\in \mathbb{Z}_{\ge 0}, \\
q &\in \mathbb{R}^3_{>0}, \\
r^{(u)} &\in \mathbb{R}^3_{>0}, \\
r^{(\Delta u)} &\in \mathbb{R}^3_{>0}.
\end{align*}

The weighting matrices are given by
\[
Q = \mathrm{diag}(10^{q}), \quad
R_u = \mathrm{diag}(10^{r^{(u)}}), \quad
R_{\Delta u} = \mathrm{diag}(10^{r^{(\Delta u)}}).
\]
Horizons are
\begin{align*}
m &= \theta_m + 1 \\
p &= \theta_p + m
\end{align*}
